Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Who we are

Metronomely (metronomely.com) is a free online metronome and is part of the Audio Tools Network. You can reach us any time at contact@metronomely.com. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens to data when you use the site.

The metronome runs in your browser

The metronome itself generates all of its audio locally on your device using your browser's Web Audio API. We do not require an account, and we do not collect or store the audio you produce. Settings such as your setlist are saved in your browser's local storage on your own device - they are not transmitted to us.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies. Some support basic site functionality and analytics, and some are placed by third-party advertising partners as described below. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings.

Advertising

We display ads to keep Metronomely free. We use Google AdSense as an advertising partner. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies and identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet. You can learn more and manage your preferences here:

  • Google ads and how data is used: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Opt out of personalized Google ads: google.com/settings/ads
  • Opt out of third-party vendor cookies: aboutads.info

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand anonymous, aggregated usage - for example, which pages are visited and roughly how long people stay. This helps us improve the tool. Google's handling of this data is covered by their privacy policy.

Affiliate disclosure

Some pages contain affiliate links, including links to products on Amazon as part of the Amazon Associates program. If you click one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the site.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal information. We do not collect account or audio data. The data involved is limited to the cookies and analytics described above.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it, and to opt out of personalized advertising using the links above. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@metronomely.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page.
